Key Oceanographic Parameters

Comprehensive monitoring of essential ocean variables

Temperature Monitoring

Continuous measurement of sea surface and subsurface temperatures across multiple depths and geographic regions.

  • Multi-depth profiling
  • Real-time data transmission
  • Thermal stratification analysis
  • Anomaly detection algorithms

Salinity & Conductivity

Precise measurement of ocean salinity patterns and electrical conductivity to understand water mass movements.

  • CTD sensor integration
  • Water mass identification
  • Mixing layer analysis
  • Freshwater flux monitoring

Ocean Currents

Advanced current velocity and direction measurement using acoustic Doppler current profilers and drift buoys.

  • ADCP measurements
  • Surface drift tracking
  • Tidal current analysis
  • Circulation modeling

Chemical Properties

Monitoring of dissolved oxygen, pH levels, nutrients, and other chemical indicators of ocean health.

  • pH and acidity monitoring
  • Dissolved oxygen levels
  • Nutrient concentration
  • Carbon cycle tracking

Satellite Observations

  • Sea level altimetry
  • Ocean color analysis
